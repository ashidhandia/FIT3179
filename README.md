## About the project

As AI capabilities advance, the public conversation focuses on what models can do — not what it costs to build them. This project visualises those hidden trade-offs across three lenses:

- **Economic scale** — National AI investment budgets compared against country GDPs
- **Model growth** — Exponential growth in parameter counts plotted against human brain capacity
- **Environmental cost** — Carbon footprints and energy consumption during intensive training runs

---

## Features

- **Scrollytelling layout** — Story-driven flow guides users from macro economic data down to environmental metrics
- **Interactive filtering** — Dynamic controls to explore the investment landscape by country and sector
- **Responsive charts** — Vega-Embed handles resize and tooltip interactions out of the box
