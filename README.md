## About the project
**Assignment constraint: All visualizations were built exclusively with Vega-Lite (no D3, no Chart.js, no custom canvas). Everything interactive was achieved within that single library.

As AI capabilities advance, the public conversation focuses on what models can do, not what it costs to build them. This project visualizes those hidden trade-offs across three lenses:

- *Global AI arms race*: countries pouring trillions into AI development, with national investment budgets that rival entire country GDPs.
- *Exponential growth of model scale*: AI parameters doubling year over year, rapidly outpacing the human brain.
- *Environmental toll*: training a single large model can dwarf an individual's lifetime carbon footprint.

The goal isn't to vilify AI, it's to ask: are we accounting for the full cost of progress?
